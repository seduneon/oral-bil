# English Study (General, IELTS, TOEFL, SAT)

## My View

The natural way I am proposing is to move gradually from General English to Exam English. 

The timeline is approximately:
1. General English: 9th grade Summer - 10th grade November
2. Exam English: 10th grade - 11th grade December

In order to do this, one needs to start surround himself with the language totally.

For example, by
1. Reading CF problem statements in English
2. Watching YouTube in English
3. Reading books / articles in English
4. Running a Telegram blog / channel in English, though it may sound cringy

## Grammar, Vocabulary, and Practice Misconception

Also, a misconception about learning English is that people perceive it as this following path:

Learning Grammar -> Then Vocabulary -> Then Practice.

However, I believe that it should be in reverse order:

Practicing -> Then Vocabulary -> Then Grammar.

## Next Steps (Homework)

So, do not waste time studying grammar in depth, because you do not have enough practical experience yet.
For now, focus on Listening and Reading. They will give you necessary vocabulary and sense of grammar, on which Writing and Speaking will be based.
